## 🔐 Create a Cohere Account & API Key

Before using CyberForge, you’ll need to create a Cohere account and generate an API key. Follow the steps below to get started:

---

### 🧭 Step 1: Navigate to the Cohere Platform

1. Go to [**cohere.com**](https://www.cohere.com)
2. Click **Sign In** at the top right

<img width="1677" alt="Screenshot 2025-06-17 at 10 51 20 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4375fe29-db91-453e-b891-b1fd0a03645d" />

---

### ✍️ Step 2: Sign Up

* If you **don’t** have an account, select **Sign up** and enter your email and password
* Alternatively, sign in with a partner provider; Google or GitHub

<img width="1702" alt="Screenshot 2025-06-17 at 10 51 58 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8fd96c16-4209-476b-97cc-88e9353a0de5" />

*Continue with a partner provider*

<img width="1687" alt="Screenshot 2025-06-17 at 10 53 13 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1ffd2880-16a0-4e1a-8d2c-5ffc9897a268" />

*Sign up with new account* 

---

### 📩 Step 3: Verify Your Email

*Now, you'll receive an email sent by Cohere to verify your email.

<img width="1698" alt="Screenshot 2025-06-17 at 10 53 46 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1e640753-0f3a-43c7-be7e-d4b8928ad0c6" />

* Open your email inbox and click the **Confirm your email** button in the email.

<img width="1371" alt="Screenshot 2025-06-17 at 11 01 37 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4acd16f5-763a-41ac-b7cc-76cef01bd5ba" />

---

### 👤 Step 4: Fill Out Profile Info

* Enter your **first and last name**

  <img width="669" alt="Screenshot 2025-06-17 at 11 02 28 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6361edec-597c-4737-97f9-aa7b24ae02f2" />

* Select your **role**

  <img width="639" alt="Screenshot 2025-06-17 at 2 24 04 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e714196c-01a3-422f-86cf-eba01c1ee62a" />

* Choose how you plan to use Cohere and click **Submit**

  <img width="634" alt="Screenshot 2025-06-17 at 2 24 26 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8fb2d489-2af2-4a40-830e-89aea21ec16e" />

---

### ⚙️ Step 5: Navigate to the Dashboard

Once you're inside your dashboard:

* Select **API Keys** from the sidebar.

<img width="1703" alt="Screenshot 2025-06-17 at 2 24 53 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/339667e6-1cb9-4e9e-abd1-dd0f3d02b4c0" />

---

### 🧪 Step 6: Use a Trial Key (Limited Usage)

* Under the **Trial Keys** section, copy your **free key** to get started with CyberForge right away.

<img width="1424" alt="Screenshot 2025-06-17 at 2 27 23 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f73aba7e-d681-4885-a9b0-ea0d2773bf31" />

---

### 🚀 Step 7: Create a Production Key (Strongly Recommended)

* Click to **create a new Production Key** for higher usage limits. For full compatiability with CyberForge, a production key is strongly recommended.

<img width="840" alt="Screenshot 2025-06-17 at 2 42 50 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7a9a352a-0282-4899-9fea-cc76dd0cb423" />

* Answer the onboarding questions:

✅ Select **Yes** to the safety and legal questions, and please input a company name

<img width="612" alt="Screenshot 2025-06-17 at 2 28 50 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/45a4bbf3-1b93-49a8-8961-8e10b8de6476" />

🚫 Select **No** when asked about the following use cases 

<img width="617" alt="Screenshot 2025-06-17 at 2 29 18 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2f93e269-b188-455d-886b-0b40918acc14" />

---

### 💳 Step 8: Add Billing Info

* Enter your **credit card** details to activate your production key.

<img width="632" alt="Screenshot 2025-06-17 at 2 37 14 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e2637f42-23a2-40f4-a3fb-bc6fe71d7ac1" />

* Name your key and click **Generate Production key** to generate it.

<img width="636" alt="Screenshot 2025-06-17 at 2 40 37 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9b98af3e-f796-4503-b3dc-12b4ac71d332" />

---

### 📋 Step 9: Copy and Store Your Key

> ⚠️ **Important:** Once generated, make sure to **copy your key immediately**. You won’t be able to view it again.

<img width="631" alt="Screenshot 2025-06-17 at 2 44 56 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b38394c1-8d21-4e1a-8bba-950c6f49d599" />

### ✅ You're Ready!

You can now use your Cohere API key in CyberForge and take full advantage of all features!
